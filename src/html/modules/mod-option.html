Critical Operations Technician

CHICAGO

STACK is looking for a Critical Operations Technician (COT) to support a local data center campus. As a member of STACK’s Critical Operations team, the COT must possess strong knowledge of maintaining the mechanical, electrical, fire life safety, base building and other various internal data center systems. The COT will also be focused on the execution of programs related to Safety, Team, Availability, Communication and Knowledge. The COT will be responsible for day-to-day operations of the data center.
